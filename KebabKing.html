Kebab King

Kebab King is a, newly opened restaurant which located at Dehiwala with Takeaway, Dine-in & Online Orders such as uber

With this application you can

  • Add & Manage Food Items
  • View Income & Profit
  • Manage Kitchen Order Notes
  • Issue Invoice
  • Send Daily Report
  • Manage Delivery Services
  • Manage System Users
  • Manage Customers
  • Can Manage Daily Expanses
